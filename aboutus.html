About Matara District Chamber of Commerce

MDCC operates with the objectives of promoting entrepreneur development services, enterprise information services, marketing services, promoting awareness and ethics amongst the business community in the Matara District.

The work of the Chamber includes organizing networking opportunities, educational forums, trade events, as well as publishing the chamber newsletter and website to give local business organizations worldwide exposure.

The chamber helps small businesses and companies to grow and save money. Its staff handles dozens of phone inquiries, mail requests, e-mail messages and business meetings each month. The Chamber has formed alliances with business leaders, trade associations, sister chambers, government agencies, civic officials & foreign partners to accomplish its goals of providing business & community orientated services.

The work of the Chamber helps to improve the business climate and quality of life in the Matara District for residents, workers and visitors. It provides valuable services to over 600 business organizations that represent the backbone of the Matara business community.
